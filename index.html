The SAT test is one of the crucial extrance exams for colleges all over America. Many colleges and universities look at SAT/ACT scores as a reference for a student's readiness for College/University when picking an applicant for their schools. The SAT test has two required sections: Evidence-Based Reading and Writing, and Mathematics. The scores from each required section can range from 200 to 800, so the best possible total score would be 1600.
